Welcome to Bohocove Resort

Founded in 1985 and fully renovated in 2024, Bohocove blends timeless charm with modern comfort. With over 20 rooms, a private beach, an outdoor pool, and a restaurant, we offer the perfect coastal getaway for relaxation and enjoyment.

Bohocove takes its name from a fusion of "Boho," reflecting the bohemian style known for its freshness, creativity, and free-spirited vibe, and "Cove," symbolizing a tranquil and intimate retreat. Bohocove captures the essence of peaceful seclusion and natural beauty. It offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and bohemian charm, inviting guests to unwind and embrace a lifestyle inspired by relaxation and artistic expression.
